Further information

All "cans" on this page refer to standard 500ml Irish Beverage containers of 4.5% alcohol. This is equal to about 19g of ethanol per serving. One shot of 40% alcohol contains around 12g of ethanol. To make calculations for shots instead, multiply the number of cans by a factor of 1.5.
